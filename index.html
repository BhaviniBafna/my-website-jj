About J+S+™ (Jai Jinendra Skill n Service)

J+S+™ is built on one promise: early STEM and future-ready learning that actually sticks. For over 10 years, our team has guided children from Pre-K to Grade 9 in Math, Coding, Robotics, and problem solving. We use a highly visual, hands-on approach so kids don’t just memorize — they understand, apply, and explain. Our curriculum is closely aligned with school topics and prepares students for competitive expectations. Parents choose us because of our small batch size, our personal feedback, and our consistency.

What makes us different

  • Visual, hands-on learning — math, logic, and robotics taught through real objects, demos, circuits, and sketches.
  • No mugging up — we build thinking, not copying. Kids explain in their own words.
  • Aligned with school — follows current school topics and supports competitive exam foundations.
  • Expert guidance — sessions are designed and led by a team with 10+ years of teaching and mentoring experience.

Parents at the center

You get visibility into progress, timely feedback, and make‑up options when life happens. We aim to be your partner in your child’s learning journey.

Outcomes you can see

Confidence with numbers and logic, a portfolio of mini‑projects, and steady improvement in schoolwork and tests.
